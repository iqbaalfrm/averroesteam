Privacy Policy - Averroes

Effective date: 14 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Averroes collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name and email address when you register.
  • Learning activity data: class progress, quiz answers, and certificate history.
  • App preferences: language and notification settings you choose.
  • Basic technical data: server logs may record IP address, timestamps, and request metadata.

We do not request precise location or access contacts.

How We Use Information

  • Provide login and account management.
  • Deliver learning content and store progress.
  • Send notifications you enable.
  • Improve stability, security, and support.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with service providers only to operate the app:

  • Backend hosting and database providers.
  • Email provider for OTP delivery (forgot password).
  • Third-party AI provider for the Chatbot feature (only when you use the Chatbot).
  • Prayer time API provider (city name only, no personal identifiers).

Data Retention

We keep data while your account is active. You can request deletion by contacting us.

Security

We use reasonable security measures, including hashed passwords and access controls. No system is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.

Contact

Email: support@averroes.app

Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the effective date above.
